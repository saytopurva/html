Heading Level 1

Tag is used for the main heading of a page,represnting the most important content.use this sparingly,typically only once per page.

Heading Level 2

The tag is used for subheading,typically to divide section under the main heading.

Heading Level 3

This tag is used for subsection under it.It helps further organize content.

Heading level 4

this tag is used for smaller subsection,typically for detailing specific point under h3

Heading level 5

this tag is used for even less prominant section or sub-sections of very minor details.

Heading level 6

This tag is used for least important ,used for minor details.
