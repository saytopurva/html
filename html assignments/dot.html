Welcome to the Department of Technology

Savitribai Phule Pune University's Department of Technology aims at providing a research platform for solving cutting edge industry-relevant research problems. The department runs industry-University sponsored M.Tech-Ph.D. integrated programs. Four Technology boards have been established under the umbrella of 'Faculty of Technology', namely Chemical and Biotechnology (CB), Computer and Information Technology (CI), Electrical and Electronics Technology (EE) and Mechanical and Materials Technology (MM). The Department of Technology promotes industry-academia interaction in the true sense, by providing a formal platform for doctoral candidates to carry out industry-sponsored research projects. The sponsoring industries partner with the University to define quality research problems of relevance. The academic research guides ensure research potential and industry research guides ensure the relevance of the research topic, thus guiding doctoral students to carve significant research solutions.
